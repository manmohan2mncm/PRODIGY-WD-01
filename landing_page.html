About Us

Founded in 1985 by childhood friends Jake Morrison and Lisa Patel, Cool Wheels has grown from a small startup in Maplewood into a global leader in the toy industry. Known for our innovative designs and high-quality craftsmanship, we have captivated the imaginations of children and collectors around the world.

Today, Cool Wheels stands as a testament to the enduring appeal of toy vehicles, combining nostalgic charm with modern ingenuity. The brand's dedication to innovation and excellence ensures that it remains a beloved name in the world of toy cars for generations to come.
